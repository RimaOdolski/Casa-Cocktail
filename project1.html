Cocktail Recipe

Mexican Martini


INGREDIENTS

Tequila: 1oz

Triple Sec 1oz

Tequila: 1oz

Sprite: 1oz

Orange Juice 1oz

Olive Juice 0.5oz

Lime juice 0.5oz


DIRECTIONS

1. Fill shaker with ice

2. Fill shake with ingredients

3. Shake the shaker vigorously to mix ingredients

4. Pour drink into martini glass, use strainer to keep ice out of glass

5. Garnish with 3 olives on a toothpick
